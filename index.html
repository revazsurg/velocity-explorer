About this visualization: Market velocity measures how frequently vehicles change ownership, calculated as ownership transfers divided by active registered vehicles, normalized to a monthly rate. Market share represents each segment's proportion of total active vehicles. Data sourced from the Georgian Ministry of Internal Affairs vehicle registration database (police.ge), covering 2017–2025. Dot size reflects fleet size (number of active vehicles). Quadrant boundaries are set at the global median across all time periods. Use the period slider to track market dynamics over time, drill down from brands to models to manufacture years, and Ctrl+scroll to zoom (double-click to reset).
